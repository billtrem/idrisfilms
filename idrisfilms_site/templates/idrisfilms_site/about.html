About

Idris Films is an independent film studio founded by Will Tremlett, based between Machynlleth and Manchester.

We work across film production, commissions and distribution, collaborating with a trusted network of freelancers to deliver projects of different scales. Our work includes specialist production services for original film projects, commissioned films for businesses and organisations, and support for bringing films to audiences through community cinema.
